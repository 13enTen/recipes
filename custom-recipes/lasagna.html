Lasagna

Lasagna Recipe Description

To make a simple lasagna, first cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Prepare a meat sauce by browning ground beef with garlic and onion, then simmer with tomato sauce, herbs, salt, and pepper. In a separate bowl, mix ricotta cheese with an egg and season with salt and pepper. Layer a baking dish with the meat sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, and shredded mozzarella cheese, repeating the layers and finishing with mozzarella. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 45 minutes until bubbly and golden. Let it cool slightly before serving.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 can of crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans of tomato sauce
  • 2 cans of tomato paste
  • 2 tablespoons of white sugar
  • Black pepper and salt
  • Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
