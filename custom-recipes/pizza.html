Pizza

Pizza Recipe Description

To make a simple pizza, first prepare the dough by mixing yeast, warm water, sugar, flour, olive oil, and salt, then let it rise for 1-2 hours. Prepare the sauce by combining tomato sauce with minced garlic, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Roll out the dough, spread the sauce on top, add mozzarella cheese and desired toppings, then bake in a preheated oven at 475°F for 10-15 minutes. Enjoy your homemade pizza!

Ingredients

Dough:

  • 2 ¼ tsp yeast
  • 1 ½ cups warm water
  • 3 ½ to 4 cups flour
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tsp sugar
  • 2 tsp salt

Sauce:

  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • 1 tsp basil
  • Salt and pepper

Toppings:

  • 2 cups mozzarella
  • 2 cups mushrooms

Steps

  1. Mix 2 ¼ tsp yeast, 1 ½ cups warm water, 2 tsp sugar. Let sit 5 min.
  2. Combine 3 ½ cups flour, 2 tsp salt. Add yeast mix, 2 tbsp olive oil. Knead.
  3. Let rise 1-2 hours.
  4. Mix 1 cup tomato sauce, 2 minced garlic cloves, 1 tsp oregano, 1 tsp basil, salt, and pepper.
  5. Preheat oven to 475°F.
  6. Roll out dough, spread sauce, add 2 cups mozzarella, and toppings.
  7. Bake 10-15 minutes.
