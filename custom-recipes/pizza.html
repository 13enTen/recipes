Pizza Recipe Description

To make a delicious homemade pizza, youâ€™ll need a few basic ingredients and steps. Begin by preparing the dough with yeast, warm water, sugar, flour, olive oil, and salt, letting it rise for about one to two hours. While the dough rises, mix together a simple tomato sauce with garlic, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Once the dough has risen, roll it out and spread the sauce evenly over the surface. Sprinkle a generous amount of mozzarella cheese on top, and add your favorite toppings such as pepperoni, mushrooms, or bell peppers. Bake the assembled pizza in a preheated oven for ten to fifteen minutes, until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly. Let it cool for a few minutes, slice, and enjoy your homemade pizza!

Ingredients

  • Yeast
  • Water
  • Flour
  • Olive Oil
  • Sugar
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Mix yeast, water, and sugar
  2. Combine flour with yeast mix and add olive oil.
  3. Let rise for one hour
  4. Mix tomato sauce with garlic
  5. Preheat oven
  6. Roll out dough, spread sauce, add two cups mozzarella, and toppings
  7. Bake for ten to fifteen minutes
